We here at Gesture.inc provide education to the mute and deaf kids all around the world. We support them by proving finacial, medical and nutritional goods. Our orphanages scattered all around the world take care of fostred kids and provide them with love.

"Disability need not be an obstacle to Success"-Stephen Hawking
English theoretical physicist

Gesture.inc is a non-profit company serving for the good of kids with disabilities. From 2011 our team has been working to provide quality education and shelter to these poor or fostered kids. Our Specialists take care of these kids to make them feel welcome and ensure their good mental, nutritional and physical health. We strongly believe that disabilities aren't one's bad abilities, disabilities are different abilities, and should never prevent one from reaching success.
